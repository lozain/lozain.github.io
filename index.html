This dedication extends to implementing robust automated testing strategies for all our solutions and providing comprehensive support for full-stack development, agile methodologies, and technical leadership throughout your digital transformation journey.

Web App MVP Strategy

Embrace an Agile MVP strategy for your web application, where you figure out precisely what your business needs, fail faster, build and test, and avoid wasting time on polished features; if something doesn't work, pivot and try other approaches for rapid innovation and market success.

Optimal Tooling for Company Skillset

Streamline your web app development by finding the right tooling based on your company's skillset and industry standards, ensuring efficient utilization of resources and adherence to best practices.

Accessibility

Craft accessibility-focused web applications by leveraging specialized tools like screen readers, keyboard navigators, and color contrast checkers, ensuring inclusivity and compliance with accessibility standards.

Technologies

We are seasoned frontend development specialists with deep expertise in JavaScript, TypeScript, React, and Next.js. Our focus lies in crafting user-friendly interfaces, leveraging tools like Storybook, styled-components, and SCSS, and excelling in the creation of comprehensive design systems.

Testing and Code Quality Specialists

We prioritize code quality and reliability, using Jest, React Testing Library, Cypress, and Playwright for testing. ESLint and Prettier maintain clean code, and we practice Test-Driven Development (TDD) rigorously. Our aim is flawless functionality and top-notch performance.

Development Tools

Our toolbox comprises Redux Toolkit, Node.js, Webpack, and GraphQL for robust applications. Git, PNPM, NPM, and Yarn manage version control and packages. We excel in collaborative, agile environments, leveraging Jira and GitHub for project management. Visual Studio Code is our coding hub, where we thrive in A/B testing, automation, and streamlining complex tasks.
