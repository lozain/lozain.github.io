Graphic Designer

The worker will be required to perform all basic duties and responsibilities associated with the job role, including studying design briefs and determining requirements. The worker will be responsible for creating initial concepts, presenting ideas, and creating content that brings customers' brands to life, inspiring and supporting our new and existing customers. They will also need to amend designs based on feedback and ensure final graphics and layouts are visually appealing and on-brand.
The job requires a Graphic Designer with a proven three years of experience. The ideal candidate should possess the ability to work methodically and meet deadlines effectively.

Personal Assistant

The worker will be required to perform all basic duties and responsibilities associated with the job role, including liaising with clients, suppliers, and other staff. They will need to manage the diary, schedule meetings and appointments, act as a point of contact between the Director and staff and clients, screen and direct phone calls, and distribute correspondence, among other tasks. Additionally, they should handle requests and queries appropriately, make travel arrangements according to the Director's diary, and take dictation and minutes.
The job requires proven personal assistant experience, preferably in the IT industry. Proficiency in English is essential, and fluency in other languages such as Punjabi, Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, and Gujarati is highly desirable.
