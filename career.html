Junior Graphic Designer

We are seeking a dynamic Junior Graphic Designer with a strong foundation in graphic design principles, proficiency in tools like Sketch, and a keen interest in infographics and basic video editing. The ideal candidate will possess excellent communication skills to interact with clients, collaborate closely with developers to understand business requirements, and deliver visually compelling designs across digital and print platforms. If you are a creative individual looking to contribute your skills in a collaborative environment and eager to grow in the field of graphic design, we invite you to join our team.

Personal Assistant

We are in search of a motivated and recent graduate from the UK to join our team as a Personal Assistant. The ideal candidate will possess excellent English communication skills, with a preference for multilingual abilities, and a basic understanding of IT and computer systems. Responsibilities include preparing documentation for clients, managing client communications, and providing essential administrative support. This role offers a unique opportunity for the right candidate to immerse themselves in the IT business, fostering a platform to kickstart a rewarding career in the dynamic field of Information Technology. If you are an enthusiastic individual ready to learn and contribute to a thriving team, we encourage you to apply for this exciting position.

Quality Assurance Engineer

We are seeking a skilled Quality Assurance Engineer to join our team and ensure the seamless functionality and high-quality performance of our websites. The ideal candidate will have a keen eye for detail, adept at identifying and tracking bugs, and proficient in writing end-to-end tests, preferably using tools like Playwright. Experience in cross-browser testing is crucial, and an understanding of SEO scoring through tools such as Web Vitals and Lighthouse is highly desirable. This role offers the opportunity to collaborate closely with experienced software engineers, providing a learning environment for continuous professional growth. If you are passionate about delivering top-notch user experiences and have a knack for uncovering software flaws, we invite you to apply for this challenging and rewarding position as a Quality Assurance Engineer.
